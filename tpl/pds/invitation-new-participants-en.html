About the survey
The main objective of the ZEW Private Debt Survey is to enhance the knowledge of market participants about the European private debt market. The focus of the survey are overall conditions of the European Corporate Direct Lending, Real Estate Debt, and Infrastructure Debt markets.

About ZEW Mannheim
The ZEW - Leibniz Centre for European Economic Research in Mannheim is a non-profit and independent institute with the legal form of a limited liability company (GmbH). ZEW is a member of the Leibniz Association. We are one of Germany's leading economic research institutes and enjoy a strong reputation throughout Europe. Our ZEW Indicator of Economic Sentiment is one of the closest followed indicators of investor sentiment for the German economy.

Data protection
Of course, we will treat all responses to this survey as strictly confidential and in compliance with the GDPR. We will only pass on aggregated, anonymized responses to our cooperation partner Prime Capital AG, who will prepare the document featuring the survey results.
